Resume Writing Tips
Tailor Your Resume

Customize your resume for each job application by highlighting relevant skills and experiences that match the job description.

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers and percentages to demonstrate your impact (e.g., "Increased sales by 20%" rather than "Increased sales").

Use Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong action verbs like "Developed," "Implemented," "Led," or "Achieved" to make your accomplishments stand out.
